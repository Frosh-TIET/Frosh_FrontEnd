Facilities

Activity Space

Activity Space is a well-equipped, air-conditioned space that provides students with a comfortable and productive environment for collaboration and teamwork.

Banks & ATMs

Banks and ATMs TIET offers convenient financial services to students and staff with two banks SBI and ICICI; and ATMs from SBI, ICICI, PNB and HDFC located on campus. They are accessible to all the students during their working hours and are located in COS and near Streat Cafe.

Rickshaw Facility

E-rickshaws are available to help commuters get around campus easily. They charge a fare of Rs 10 per head, and accept both cash and digital payments, making it convenient for passengers to pay.

Hunger Box App

Sodexo is the exclusive caterer who has been entrusted for numerous hostels and the canteen on the vast campus of TIET. Sodexo has implemented a new policy that allows users to place digital orders easily through the “HungerBox app”.

Post Office

The campus has a government-managed post office, India Post, conveniently located near the SBI bank.This reliable centre offers a comprehensive range of postal services, promoting seamless communication and simplifying mailing within the campus community.Its presence plays a crucial role in augmenting convenience, connectivity, and effective communication for individuals on campus.
