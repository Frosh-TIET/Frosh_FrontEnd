Academic Blocks

Admin Block

The institute's central building stands as the focal point, housing the Director's office, the Registrar's office, and serving as the administrative hub of the entire institution. Conveniently situated in close proximity, it serves as the go-to destination for any individual seeking clarification or assistance regarding administrative matters.

B-Block

Situated within the Institute, this building stands as one of the oldest and most esteemed academic structures, serving as the designated venue for department-specific classes and sessional exams. Opposite G block, this historic edifice holds a profound significance in the institution's legacy, symbolizing its rich heritage and academic tradition.

TAN Building

TAN Building Teaching and Academic Networking is a prestigious and renowned location that serves as a centre for various academic endeavours, hosting tutorials for a wide array of subjects. Additionally, it also serves as a versatile venue where numerous societies conduct their meetings and organize events like hackathons and Model United Nations (MUNs). Its multifunctional nature makes it a cherished hub for intellectual and extracurricular activities within the institute.

LT Halls

The Lecture Theatre, commonly referred to as "LT," is a prominent venue within the institute where diverse lectures on various subjects are conducted. With its significantly large seating capacity, it accommodates a considerable audience during these academic sessions. Additionally, the Lecture Theatre serves as a prestigious platform for hosting guest lectures, further enriching the academic environment.

LP

The Lecture Pavilion, known as the "LP," stands as a venue where the majority of lectures are held in accordance with the schedule. Adjacent to the LP, there lies a spacious and picturesque LP lawn, where students often gather to relax and relish the scenic view before or after their classes. This tranquil setting provides an ideal space for students to unwind, fostering a conducive learning environment within the institute.
