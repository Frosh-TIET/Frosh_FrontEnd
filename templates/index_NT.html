NON-TECH WORKSHOPS

Kalamanch Acting

This workshop conducts various activities pertaining to drama and the skill of acting. This workshop is for those drama enthusiasts who show their love for the stage.

Groove

Workshop conducted for those students who can rhythmically and express their emotions through western form of dance.

Verse

This workshop is for those students strung up on words, images and the rhythm of Poetry. This includes Rap Writing, Storytelling & Poetry.

Music

This workshop is for the music lovers who learn the pure expression of sound and melodies to produce the art of music.

AAKRITI

This workshop is for the creative thinkers who can paint their ideas by using their limitless imagination.

UDDARI

Workshop conducted for those students who can rhythmically and express their emotions through Punjabi Dance form.

Digital Arts

This workshop is for those who have highly imaginative minds and have the ability to convert reality into virtuality.

Innaayat

A workshop that celebrates grace and agility.This workshop has been designed to teach you the different forms of classical dance.

Cogitation

This workshop is for those who have the drive and ability to convey their ideas while also inspiring and influencing the audience through their speaking skills.
